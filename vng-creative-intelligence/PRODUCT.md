# PRODUCT.md — VNG P5 Creative Performance Intelligence

## Register
product (data-dense decision dashboard) — with one brand-register moment: the skin-generation reveal and 3D stage, which is demo theater and may exceed product restraint.

## What it is
An agentic UA (user-acquisition) intelligence dashboard for VNG Games, built for Agentic AI Build Week 2026 (Jul 8–12, HCMC). It joins ad-platform performance with predicted LTV, clusters creative themes by high-value-player share, recommends the next creative direction with a defensible holdout backtest (baselines, CI, p-value), then drives a generation pipeline: skin concepts → reception simulation (MiroShark) → best pick → 3D model preview.

## Users
1. **Hackathon judges** (VNG Games execs + GenAI Fund) watching a 3-minute stage demo on a projector, then poking at the deployed app on their own devices. They reward: demo wow, ROI on pLTV-weighted ROAS, visible agentic depth, defensible evidence.
2. **The pitch persona:** a VNG UA director deciding which creative theme to fund next; reads charts fast, distrusts unexplained numbers.

## Environment / scene
Dim conference hall, big projector, 3 minutes of stage time; then judges' laptops/phones. The demo climax is a generated game-skin spinning in a 3D viewer.

## Tone
Confident analyst, not hype. Every number is traceable (methodology, CI, p-value, live/mock provenance badges are product features). Honesty labels ("mock", "fallback") are first-class, never hidden.

## Brand cues
VNG Games = Vietnamese gaming publisher; the flagship recommended theme in the sample data is "Cyberpunk Premium / Neon Mecha / Ronin". Game-art energy is available as flavor, but the credibility layer (charts, backtest) must read as rigorous intelligence, not game UI.

## Anti-references (what this must NOT look like)
- Generic AI-scaffold dashboard: identical white cards, 8px radius, gray borders, system font, no hierarchy (the current state — being replaced).
- "Crypto/gaming neon on black" template: purple-blue gradients, glow-everything. If dark, it must be a considered dark, not the category reflex.
- SaaS hero-metric template (big number + small label + gradient accent, four in a row).

## Strategic principles
1. The evidence is the hero: the backtest + baseline table is the winning slide; design must make it legible from the back of a room.
2. One spectacular moment (generation reveal → 3D stage) placed deliberately; everything else quiet and disciplined.
3. Provenance (live vs mock vs fallback) is part of the trust story — visible, honest, never decorative.
4. Charts follow dataviz discipline: fixed categorical hue order, one axis, thin marks, direct labels, recessive grid.
